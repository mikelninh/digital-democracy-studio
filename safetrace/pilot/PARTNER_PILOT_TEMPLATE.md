## Success measures

Record the baseline and observed SafeTrace result for:

- time to complete the defined workflow;
- material claims with source provenance;
- material claims human-reviewed;
- false entity connections;
- corrections;
- privacy or security incidents;
- reviewer comprehension;
- partner usefulness rating.

## Escalation

Define contacts and response times for security incidents, privacy incidents, evidence-integrity concerns, legally sensitive claims and risk to a victim or witness.

## Go/no-go approval

A live pilot begins only after security, privacy, legal/editorial and partner-operation owners have approved the same scoped version of this document. Any material scope expansion requires a new approval.
