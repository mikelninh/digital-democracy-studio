# SafeTrace Wohlstandslabor v0.1

A public-source, browser-local prototype for evaluating policy through five dimensions:

1. simplicity;
2. productivity;
3. fairness;
4. resilience;
5. evidence quality.

## Core rule

Perspective filters may change ordering and questions. They must never change facts, sources or verdict text.

## Initial policy modules

- Citizen Capital / private-pension reform;
- Energy abundance;
- Government as anchor customer;
- AI and robotics dividend.

## Two surfaces

- **Labor:** facts, incentives, beneficiaries, payers, risks, alternatives and visible sources.
- **3-minute brief:** the same evidence prioritized by life situation rather than party affiliation.

## Boundaries

- static public-source snapshot dated 2026-07-24;
- no live telemetry;
- browser-local preferences and calculator only;
- no investment advice;
- no party persuasion or hidden personalization;
- not a published SafeTrace case until evidence-vault and human-review gates are completed.
