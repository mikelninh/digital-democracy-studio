Method and boundaries

Brave enough to investigate. Disciplined enough not to prejudge.

SafeTrace is designed to help human investigators structure unorganised evidence—not to automate guilt.

Verified fact

Directly supported by a primary official record or independently verifiable source.

Court-established

Established by a judgment, with appeal and finality status shown.

Official allegation

A charge, investigation or official claim that remains unresolved.

Analytical red flag

A documented pattern or inconsistency that warrants examination but proves nothing by itself.

Unresolved gap

Information expected for accountability that is missing, withheld, fragmented or not machine-readable.

Corrected or disproved

A visible correction record; SafeTrace never quietly deletes inconvenient errors.
