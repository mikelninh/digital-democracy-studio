What changed

A shared language for the entire investigation.

Case Charter

Every case needs a bounded question, public-interest rationale, owner, risks, expected sources and prohibited methods before work begins.

Unified provenance

Sources and immutable snapshots are linked to evidence anchors; material claims cannot exist without traceable support.

Human publication gates

Contradictions, sensitivity, legal review and right of reply are enforced as data rules rather than editorial promises.

Bounded agent tasks

AI tasks record purpose, tools, model, prompt version, cost ceiling, data-zone ceiling and required human approver.

Migration, not replacement

Adapters preserve the meaning of Source Engine, Political Money, Review Desk, Arms Monitor, Monitoring, Case Packs and Law Fairness.

Compatibility controls

A generated JSON Schema and compatibility checker prevent silent removal of required fields, statuses or safeguards.
