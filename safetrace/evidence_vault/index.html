What is enforced

The source layer is now a system of record.

Reviewed registry

Stable IDs, source rank, cadence, connector, parser, content type, retention policy and named human approval.

Immutable receipts

Raw and normalized hashes, parser versions, registry revision and previous-receipt hash form a tamper-evident chain.

Transformation manifests

Every parse, extraction, redaction and export records inputs, outputs, tools, parameters and required approval.

Health alerts

Moved, unavailable, changed and unexpected-content sources are distinguished without changing public status automatically.

Retention controls

Originals are protected; derived deletion requires approval, reason and a visible tombstone.

Verified recovery

Backups enumerate every path and hash. Restore verifies the manifest and every entry before acceptance.
