v0.9 Security & Governance

Public and synthetic evaluation: READY
Default-deny roles, human publication gates, data boundaries, tamper-evident audit events and measurable release checks are implemented.
Restricted partner or victim-sensitive data: BLOCKED
A named partner, production hosting architecture, independent security assessment, DPIA/lawful basis, retention agreement and qualified legal/editorial approval are still required.

Controls

  • No autonomous guilt decisions, publication or referrals.
  • Unknown roles receive no permission.
  • Public/synthetic mode prohibits personal, victim and witness data.
  • Audit events form a SHA-256 hash chain.
  • Every readiness control has an owner, evidence and explicit blocker.
