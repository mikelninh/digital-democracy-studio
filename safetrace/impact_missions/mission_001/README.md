# SafeTrace Impact Mission 001

## Gute Arbeit, weniger Armut, schneller Wind

This mission turns three official indicators into a bounded public action experiment:

1. **Arbeitslosenquote:** 6.2% in June 2026.
2. **Armutsgefährdungsquote:** 16.1% in 2025.
3. **Wind an Land:** 68.1 GW installed at end-2025 versus 115 GW target for 2030.

## What it does

- preserves source, period, definition and uncertainty for each indicator;
- explicitly rejects an unproven causal chain;
- proposes three falsifiable impact hypotheses;
- gives Michael five personal actions with visible proof requirements;
- exports a browser-local progress receipt and one-page Markdown brief;
- sends no telemetry and triggers no external action.

## First impact contract

The first cycle succeeds only when it produces:

- at least 10 readers;
- at least 3 external challenges;
- at least 1 real expert or organisational conversation;
- a public findings note explaining what changed and what remains unproven.

The mission itself cannot authorize publication. Human review remains mandatory.
