What is enforced

A claim is a workflow, not a paragraph.

Versioned wording

Every version preserves exact text, evidence state, author, limitations and ancestry.

Vault-backed evidence

Publishable evidence links must match a Source Registry identity, Vault receipt, object hash and exact anchor.

Dynamic review graph

Evidence, red-team and publication gates always apply; identity, legal, reply and correction gates appear when needed.

Reviewer separation

Sensitive claims and final material publication cannot be approved solely by their researcher.

Contradiction handling

Red-team review must explicitly address contradicting evidence before approval.

Visible invalidation

A corrected claim makes every publication using its previous version stale until a fresh review completes.
